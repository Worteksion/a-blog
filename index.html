Trivia

By Arttu

The idea of the picture is to be a reminder that you can try something over and over and keep failing, because you always learn something in the process. You also need to find only one working solution to any given problem (something something lightbulbs). The word crash and the picture above were chosen as a homage to the fact that when coding, something crashing is probably one of the more common problems(be it the code, the computer or your mental health).
